## Introduction

Welcome to my new blog! This post details the architectural decisions and implementation challenges of building this site.

### The Stack

The core stack is **Next.js 14** for the framework, **Chakra UI** paired with **Tailwind CSS** for styling, **Contentlayer** for MDX content, and **React Three Fiber (R3F)** for the 3D interactive hero background.
